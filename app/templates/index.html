Welcome to the Treasure Valley Water Information Center

Democratic Science in Action


Here you can run simulations of different Treasure Valley futures. Although we may differ in why we think it's happening, the data shows that less snow is falling in the Sawtooth Mountains. We need to understand if this will result in less water for Boise and the surrouding Treasure Valley area. Even if this does impact water availability for our area, it may not be of much consequence if agricultural use continues to diminish as urban and suburban development displaces agriculture in the Treasure Valley.

Here you can learn more about the socio-economic-hydrologic system of the Treasure Valley, learn about how Boise State University researchers are working to understand the problem, and even run your own version of the models that Boise State University researchers have developed for this problem.
